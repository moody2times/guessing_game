how to play

  • Choose a number from 1 - 20
  • After choosing a number press the check button to check your answer
  • If your answer is correct, you win. If your answer is wrong you lose a point
  • You start the game with 7 points.
  • If your points count down to zero, that's game over!
  • You can ask for a hint but you will lose points when you do.
  • If you use hint 1, you will lose 1 health point. And if you use hint 2, you lose 2 points.
  • You can only use each hint once during the game.
  • You cannot use hint 1 when your health points is 1, and you cannot use hint 2 when your health points is 2.
